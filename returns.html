Return Policy

We want you to love your VogueThreads purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, you can return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

1

Initiate Return

Start your return online through your account or using your order number and email address.

2

Pack Items

Include all original tags, packaging, and the return form with your items.

3

Ship Back

Use the prepaid return label provided or drop off at any authorized shipping location.

4

Get Refund

Receive your refund to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after we process your return.

Exchange Policy

Need a different size or color? We make exchanges easy. Simply follow the return process and place a new order for the item you want. We'll process your refund once we receive the returned item.

Size Exchange

If you need a different size, return the original item and place a new order for the correct size.

Color Exchange

Want a different color? Return the original item and order the color you prefer.

Item Exchange

Not loving the style? Return the item and choose something different from our collection.

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it, unworn or unused, with tags, and in its original packaging. You'll also need the receipt or proof of purchase.

Items must have original tags attached
Items must be unworn and unwashed
Original packaging must be included
Return must be initiated within 30 days

Non-Returnable Items

Certain types of items cannot be returned, like perishable goods, custom products, and personal care items. Please get in touch if you have questions or concerns about your specific item.

  • Final sale items (clearly marked at time of purchase)
  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable software products
  • Some health and personal care items
  • Items without original tags or packaging

International Returns

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using a trackable shipping service and purchasing shipping insurance. We don't guarantee that we will receive your returned item.
