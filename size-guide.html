Size Guide

Find your perfect fit with our comprehensive sizing guide

How to Measure

For the most accurate measurements, wear the undergarments you plan to wear with your clothing and have someone help you measure.

Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your bust

Waist

Measure around the narrowest part of your waist

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips

Fitting Tips

Shoulder Seams

Shoulder seams should sit right at the edge of your shoulders

Comfortable Fit

Clothing should fit comfortably without pulling or gaping

Length

Sleeves and pant legs should end at the appropriate points

Movement

You should be able to move freely in your clothing
