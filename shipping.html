Domestic Shipping

We ship to all 50 states in the US. Orders are processed within 1-2 business days and shipped from our distribution centers.

  • Standard shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Next day delivery: Available for most locations
  • Free standard shipping on orders over $75

International Shipping

We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination.

  • Customs fees and import taxes may apply
  • Delivery times: 7-21 business days depending on destination
  • Tracking information provided for all international orders
  • Some restrictions may apply to certain countries

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email to monitor your package's journey. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

Shipping FAQs

How long does it take to process an order?

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. During peak seasons or sales, processing may take slightly longer.

Do you ship on weekends?

We process and ship orders Monday through Friday. Orders placed on weekends will be processed the next business day.

Can I change my shipping address after ordering?

Address changes can only be made if the order hasn't been shipped yet. Contact our customer service team as soon as possible.

What if I'm not home when my package arrives?

Most carriers will attempt delivery 3 times before returning the package to us. You can also request package hold at a local facility.
